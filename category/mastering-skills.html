Mastering Skills

MythicMobs offers a complex skill system that allows you to create a wide variety of skills for your mobs. This chapter will really dive deep into the details of skills and how they work. By the end of this chapter, you will have the necessary knowledge to virtually create any skill you can imagine.

📄️ The Origin Targeter

Arguably the most complex Mythic targeter, @Origin is a targeter that works differently based on the context it is used in.

📄️ The Projectile

Mythic has a useful mechanic called projectile. This allows full control over projectiles in skills, allowing you to create fireballs, beams, waves, and more!
